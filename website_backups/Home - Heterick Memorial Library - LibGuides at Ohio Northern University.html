Northern Lights Cafe

Students can take pleasure in this naturally lit coffee shop or sunlight on the patio. Northern Lights - a 'Proudly Serving Starbucks' Coffee Shop features a complete coffee menu, refreshing smoothies, baked treats, and chilled products.

We Want to Hear From You!

Have an experience at the library you'd like to share? We want to hear about it! We greatly appreciate your feedback because it helps the library to make improvements to our spaces, technology, services, and collection, allowing us to better serve all of our patrons. Please use the above linked text to send us your thoughts and experiences at Heterick Memorial Library. 

First Floor Study Rooms Upgraded

The study rooms on the first floor got an upgrade! You can now wirelessly cast your laptop, tablet, or other mobile devices' screen to the televisions in Rooms 103A, 103B, 104, and 104C. Instructions on how to connect to the TV wirelessly is displayed on each screen, and help is available from the library's front desk. You can also still connect to each screen with a wired connection by using the external ports located just below each TV.

The Writing Center

The Writing Center provides support for ONU writers at every stage of the writing process. Whether you are looking for another eye to read over your already polished work or struggling to get started on a writing assignment, our trained peer tutors can help you reach the goals that you set for your writing. We serve students in all majors and subject areas, so if you have a writing-related question, schedule a one-on-one tutoring session, send us an email, or stop by our Virtual Writing Room.

Access to Top Newspapers via Heterick Library

Current students, staff, and faculty at Ohio Northern University can access top newspapers like the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and Washington Post via Heterick Library's website. Visit our Databases page or use the above linked text to start reading!

Reserve a Room

Need a space for your group to meet? Or just looking for a private space to study? Reserve a study space at the library! Each study space at Heterick contains unique features and technology to help you and your group collaborate and study. Information on what's in each room can be found by clicking the blue Info button next to each room listing.

Please note, students are limited to reserving a space for up to 4 hours once a week.
